# Demo Data Notice

## Overview

All data visible within this repository and its running demo environment is entirely fabricated for demonstration purposes. No real business, patient, provider, or insurance information is present anywhere in this codebase.

---

## What Is Fictionalized

### User Accounts
All provider accounts, clinic staff profiles, and admin accounts seeded into this system are fictional. Names, phone numbers, NPI numbers, clinic addresses, and email addresses are invented and do not correspond to real individuals or organizations.

### Manufacturers & Products
All manufacturer names, product names, Q-codes, pricing figures (pay rates, cost per cm², AOC estimates), and commission structures are illustrative only. They are modeled on the structure of real wound care CTP (Cellular/Tissue-based Product) billing but are not derived from any real vendor catalog, fee schedule, or contracted rate.

### Benefits Verification Records
All BV requests, insurance IDs, payer names, coverage determinations, and associated clinical notes in the seeded dataset are synthetic. Any resemblance to a real patient or insurance claim is coincidental.

### Orders & Order Outcomes
All product orders, fulfillment statuses, risk tier assignments, and order history records are demo entries with no commercial significance.

### Analytics & Metrics
All dashboard figures, digest summaries, and performance metrics are generated from demo seed data. They do not represent the actual business performance of any company.

### BAA Agreements
All signed Business Associate Agreements stored in the demo system are test submissions. Signatures are either placeholder values or test inputs. These documents have no legal standing.

### Email Notifications
If a live SendGrid key is configured during local development, transactional emails generated by the demo system will be real emails sent to real addresses. Avoid seeding real email addresses into a locally running instance.

---

## Medicare & Medicaid Q-Codes

Q-codes referenced in the product catalog (e.g., Q4169, Q4181, Q4203) are publicly available CMS billing codes for Cellular/Tissue-based Products. Their presence in this demo does not imply any affiliation with, endorsement by, or contracted relationship with CMS, Medicare, or any payer. Pay rate figures shown alongside Q-codes are illustrative estimates and do not reflect actual Medicare reimbursement rates.

---

## No PHI

This repository contains no Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA. All patient-adjacent data fields (wound measurements, clinical notes, insurance IDs) are populated with fabricated values intended solely to demonstrate UI and workflow functionality.

---

## Reviewer Guidance

If you are reviewing this repository for technical evaluation:

- run the seed scripts to populate a local database with demo data,
- use the seeded admin credentials to log in and explore all three role dashboards,
- submit test BV requests and orders through the provider flow to observe the full workflow,
- all records created during evaluation can be safely discarded.
